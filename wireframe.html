Recommendation: Option 2 (Dashboard-First) best serves the proposal’s stated goal — helping students "stay on top of large workloads" — because it foregrounds today’s items and upcoming deadlines instead of asking the student to scan a full grid. Option 1 (Week Grid) is the safest fallback since it most directly mirrors the Apple Calendar reference named in the proposal.
